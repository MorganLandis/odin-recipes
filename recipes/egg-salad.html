Egg Salad Recipe

Creamy egg salad.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Hard boil eggs. (Approx. 4 eggs per desired serving)
  2. Peel eggs and dice. Add to bowl.
  3. Add 1/2 cup of mayonnaise.
  4. Add 1/4 cup of mustard.
  5. Mix in salt and pepper to taste.

Serve as a sandwich or on its own.
